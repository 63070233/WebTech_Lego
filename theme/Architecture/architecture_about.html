Revel in the details

LEGO® Architecture is a delightful range of construction models that beautifully capture the authentic details of iconic buildings from around the world. Whether featuring a majestic city skyline or focused on a famous landmark, each set offers an immersive building project to help you click your stress away and create a stylish display piece to enhance any home.

The Skyline Collection

e LEGO® Architecture Skyline Collection opens a window to the most incredible cityscapes from around the globe. The buildable models showcase the best of each city’s architecture, rekindling fond memories for inhabitants and visitors, and inspiring those who dream of visiting one day.

Bucket-list places

Recreate and explore the destinations that shape your life, whether it’s an unforgettable trip, a dream vacation or the city you once called home.

Time to escape

Longing to escape the daily grind? Step back in time to those carefree childhood hours of LEGO® building as you lose yourself in the therapeutic construction process.

Designed for display

Much more than just a photo. Put your unique LEGO® keepsake on show and be transported every time you admire the view.
