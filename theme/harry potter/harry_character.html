LEGO® Harry Potter™ – Characters and Minifigures

characters

game

Harry Potter™

On his eleventh birthday, Harry Potter™ learns that he has been accepted to Hogwarts™ School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry and his friends, Ron and Hermione, brave the challenges of school life while facing the increasing threat posed by Lord Voldemort™ and his followers.

Albus Dumbledore™

Albus Dumbledore™ is the kind and wise Headmaster of Hogwarts™ School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. A powerful wizard, he is also opposed to Voldemort’s rise to power and is a great support to Harry and his friends.

Lord Voldemort™

As a young wizard, Tom Marvolo Riddle was obsessed with achieving great power and immortality. Casting off his former identity, he renamed himself Lord Voldemort and recruited an army of Dark wizards and evil creatures to serve him in conquering the Wizarding World. More commonly known as You-Know-Who or He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named, he was the most powerful Dark wizard of all time.

Hermione Granger™

One of Harry’s best friends and a fellow Gryffindor™ student, Hermione is a smart and studious pupil. Her quick thinking and impressive magic skills often aid Harry and Ron in their adventures.

Ron Weasley™

The ginger-haired and humorous Ron Weasley™ is Harry’s first friend at Hogwarts™. He may be a bit trouble-prone, but Ron is the most loyal schoolmate anyone could ask for and has an important part to play in the quest to defeat Lord Voldemort™.

Draco Malfoy™

Slytherin™ student Draco Malfoy™ is Harry’s nemesis at Hogwarts™ and is always ready to challenge him. The spoiled son of a long line of wizards and witches, Malfoy looks down on anyone with a less prestigious heritage than himself.

Severus Snape™

Professor Snape™ is the Potions Master at Hogwarts™ who gives all the witches- and wizards-in-training a hard time, but seems particularly determined to catch Harry out.

Hagrid™

Rubeus Hagrid™ is half-giant, and the one responsible for introducing Harry Potter™ to the wizarding world. A loyal friend to Harry and very fond of magical creatures, Hagrid isn’t too good at keeping secrets…
